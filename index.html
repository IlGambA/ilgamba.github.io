About Me

HI! I'm Stefano

I grew up surrounded by videogames, and as the years went by, I realized they were more than just entertainment—they were windows into incredible worlds, full of stories, characters, and details that left me amazed. That curiosity about how these worlds were made soon turned into a passion, and I knew my place was somewhere between 3D art and programming, bringing ideas to life in digital experiences.

My dream is to become a Technical Artist, a role that lets me combine two passions that might seem different but feel inseparable to me: the creative freedom of 3D art and the logical precision of programming. I love the moment when a project starts to take shape, when an idea in my head becomes something others can see, explore, and experience.

When I’m not working with engines, you’ll find me cooking, taking photos, or at the gym—these are the things that recharge me and give me inspiration beyond the screen. I believe creativity comes from a balance of discipline and passion, and I try to bring that philosophy into everything I make.
